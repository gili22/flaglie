How to play

Begin by guessing the flag that is shown, after each correct guess you will advance 1 level up, after the first level, some blocks will appear on the screen according to the level you are on. Click on the "reveal block" button to reveal 1 random block from the screen. Be aware, the more blocks you reveal, the lower the Points you will get.



Each correct guess = 200 points.

Additional points are measured by how many blocks are left on the screen. The higher the level you are on, the more Additional points you will get
